## How to Sign This Agreement

By making a Contribution to the Project, You indicate Your acceptance of this Agreement. This can be done in one of the following ways:

### Method 1: Individual Contributors (Recommended)

Add your name, email, and date to the `CONTRIBUTORS.md` file in the root of the repository as part of your first Pull Request:

```markdown
- [Your Full Name] <your.email@example.com> - YYYY-MM-DD
```

### Method 2: Corporate Contributors

If contributing on behalf of a corporation, have an authorized representative add the corporation's information to `CONTRIBUTORS.md`:

```markdown
- [Company Name] (authorized by [Full Name] <email@example.com>) - YYYY-MM-DD
```

For corporate contributions, we may require additional documentation demonstrating authorization.

### Method 3: CLA Assistant (Automated)

If the Project has enabled CLA Assistant automation, You will be prompted to sign this Agreement electronically when You submit Your first Pull Request. Follow the prompts provided by the CLA Assistant bot.
