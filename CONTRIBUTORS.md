## How to Sign

### First-Time Contributors

1. Read the [Contributor License Agreement](docs/legal/CLA.md)
2. Add your name to the appropriate section above (Individual or Corporate)
3. Include this change in your first Pull Request
4. Our automated checks will verify your CLA signature

### Corporate Contributors

If you're contributing on behalf of your employer:

1. Ensure you have authorization from your employer to contribute
2. Have an authorized representative add your company to the Corporate Contributors section
3. Include proof of authorization if requested by maintainers
4. Individual developers should still add their names to indicate who is contributing on behalf of the company

### Questions?

- **Legal Questions**: Consult your own legal counsel
- **Process Questions**: Open an issue with the `legal` label
- **Technical Questions**: See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)
